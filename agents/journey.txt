User Journey AI.Assisted CAA/MAA Approval Workbench   0. Service Overview (at a glance) The service provides a single digital approval journey that: 1. Helps operators prepare, check, and de.risk their applications before submission 2. Submits a structured, traceable package to the CAA/MAA 3. Assists regulators, without automating decisions, by: o checking clause.level compliance o surfacing gaps, risks, and extrapolations o producing a defensible decision record The AI never issues approvals. It supports human regulators and duty holders.     PHASE 1 Ń OPERATOR JOURNEY (Prepare . Improve .
