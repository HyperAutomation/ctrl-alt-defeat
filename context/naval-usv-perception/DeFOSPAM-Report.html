In-service monitoring decomposes into multiple distinct capabilities
Found by Flo — Features Analyst · decomposition_needed
CRITICAL C10 F

Detail

Section 7.7 mentions 'Drift detection, continuous re-qualification, automatic update of the evidence store on model change' but groups these as a single phase. At minimum, this decomposes into: (1) drift detection on declared variables, (2) canary deployment / staged rollout, (3) alarm escalation to the on-call team, (4) re-qualification trigger, (5) evidence store synchronization.
